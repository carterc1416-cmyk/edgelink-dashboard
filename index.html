01 · READ THE CUSTOMER BEFORE YOU SPEAK
THE RUSHED CUSTOMER
They say "I just need to pay my bill." Don't fight it — match their pace. Lead with speed, then plant the seed: "I can get you out in 2 minutes — quick question while I pull that up..." Now you have their attention.
THE PRICE SHOPPER
Never lead with price. Lead with value. "What's most important to you — reliability, data, or monthly cost?" Once you know their priority, you control the conversation. Price becomes secondary.
THE LOYAL CUSTOMER
They've been with Metro for years. They trust the brand. Use that. "Since you're already with us, let me show you what you're missing out on..." Loyalty is your opening — expand the account.
02 · THE POWER QUESTIONS — ASK THESE EVERY TIME
"Who else is on your plan?"
Opens the door to multi-line, BTS, and family upgrades.
POWER Q
"Do you have internet at home — or are you using your phone as a hotspot?"
HSI opener. Every no is a missed $10+ add-on. Ask it every single time.
POWER Q
"When was the last time you updated your phone?"
Creates urgency for upgrades. If they haven't upgraded in 2+ years, they're ready — they just need a reason.
POWER Q
"Have you heard about our protection plan?"
MIM LINES opener. Non-negotiable. The answer doesn't matter — the conversation does.
POWER Q
"What do you use your phone for the most?"
Unlocks their pain point. Heavy data user? Upgrade. Gamer? Tablet. Remote worker? HSI. Listen and solve.
POWER Q
03 · OBJECTION KILLERS — KNOW THESE COLD
❌ OBJECTION
"I'm not interested in a tablet."
✅ YOUR RESPONSE
"I get it — most people say that. But here's the thing: it's only $10 a month, it comes with its own data line so it won't slow your phone down, and if you've got kids or work remotely, you'll wonder how you went without it. Can I show you real quick?"
❌ OBJECTION
"I already have internet at home."
✅ YOUR RESPONSE
"Perfect — what are you paying for it? Because we have customers who dropped $80/month down to $25 and got the same speed. Takes 5 minutes to switch. Want me to check availability at your address?"
❌ OBJECTION
"I don't need a case, I'm careful."
✅ YOUR RESPONSE
"Every person who's ever cracked their screen said the same thing. This one's only $20 — that's less than one hour of screen repair. Let me grab it for you just in case."
❌ OBJECTION
"That's too expensive."
✅ YOUR RESPONSE
"Totally understand. What's your budget today? I want to make sure you leave protected — let me find something that works for you." Then show them THREE options at different price points.
04 · THE ELITE CLOSE — HOW TOP REPS FINISH EVERY TRANSACTION
1
ASSUME THE SALE
Don't ask "do you want accessories?" Ask "which color case do you want?" Assumptive language drives assumptive buying.
2
BUNDLE EVERYTHING
Never sell one thing. Sell the complete solution: phone + case + screen protector + charger + tablet + HSI. Every. Time.
3
URGENCY IS REAL
"This promotion ends soon." "We only have two of these left." Real urgency closes deals. Use it — but only when it's true.
4
SILENCE IS GOLDEN
After your close, stop talking. The next person who speaks loses. Let the silence work for you. Count to 10 in your head.
5
ALWAYS RECAP
Before they leave: "So today you got X, X, and X — you're fully set up." Confirmation creates satisfaction and reduces returns.

THE ELITE REP MAKES EVERY CUSTOMER A GOOD ONE."
